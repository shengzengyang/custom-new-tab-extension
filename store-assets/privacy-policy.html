Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2025

Information Collection and Use

The Custom New Tab Background extension is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data Storage

All data, including:

  • Background images and videos
  • User preferences
  • Search history

is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage APIs (local storage, IndexedDB, and Cache API). This data never leaves your device.

Network Access

The extension only makes network requests when:

  • Loading user-specified background images from URLs
  • Loading search suggestions (if enabled)

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your background images and preferences locally
  • tabs: To customize the new tab page
  • topSites: To show your frequently visited sites
  • scripting: To apply custom styles and functionality

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
